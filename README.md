# Superstore-Sales-Performance-Analysis (Using Power BI)
## Project Objective
The primary goal of this project was to transform raw transactional data into a strategic tool that identifies which product categories are driving growth and which shipping methods are most cost-effective.

## Dataset used
<a href="https://github.com/Ayush0713/Superstore-Sales-Performance-Analysis/blob/main/Superstore%20Sales%20Dashboard.pbix">Dataset</a>

## Questions KPI
- What does the Superstore generate in the overall sales volume across all regions and categories.
- How many total orders have been processed during the analyzed period.
- Is the business scaling effectively year-over-year, and by what percentage.
- How do sales and growth metrics fluctuate when comparing performance across 2015, 2016, and 2017.
- Which geographic regions (Central, East, South, West) are meeting targets.
- How do shipping choices (Standard vs. First Class) impact sales volume for different customer segments.
- What is the most common shipping method, and what percentage of the total does it represent.
